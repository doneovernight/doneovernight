Behind the Film

Romy Peters

COMMONPL4CE is shaped by Romy Peters, a photographer working between fashion, lifestyle, travel and documentary storytelling.

Her work is built around feeling: quiet direction, real presence and images that carry texture beyond the moment.

Selected projects can be captured on Kodak film to preserve grain, atmosphere and the small imperfections that make a visual feel alive.

On the Road

Rio Through Romy’s Lens

During her travels, Romy looks for real moments.

People, places and energy. Nothing staged, nothing forced.

Rio is a city that keeps giving itself to those willing to slow down and observe.

These photographs are small reminders of a place that continues to inspire her work.
