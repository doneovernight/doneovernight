Questions

Answered before you ask.

How is this different from an agency?

Agencies bill in hours and schedule in weeks. We bill in scope and deliver in nights. You don't manage account managers; you hand off a task and receive the output. No timelines, no approvals chains, no scope meetings.

Is this AI-generated slop with a premium markup?

No. Every output is driven by a senior operator using AI tooling as a force multiplier, then reviewed by a second human before it ships. If you can't tell the difference between our work and work done by a senior in-house team, that's the point.

What about confidentiality for sensitive material?

Mutual NDA before any work. Access is scoped to the assigned operator. Source files deleted 14 days post-delivery. Nothing is used for training, case studies, or marketing without written consent. See our trust page for the full policy.

Can we start small before committing?

Yes. You can start with a single standard task (€250) to see how we work, then step up to a Block Retainer once you're confident. No pressure to commit upfront.

What happens if our scope changes?

We re-quote the delta and pause until you confirm. No surprise invoices. If scope changes mean we can't hit the original deadline, we tell you before it's a problem.

Can you work in our tools?

Usually yes. Google Workspace, Notion, Figma, HubSpot, Airtable, n8n, Zapier, Make, Framer, Webflow — all standard. If you use something more niche, ask us on the discovery call.
