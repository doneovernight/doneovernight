why this is real

You have four degrees. SaiUniversity isn't a brand name, it's a self-portrait. The intellectual angle isn't a costume or an aesthetic choice, it's your literal background. The brand should look like the kind of person you actually are: educated, observant, online, feminine, soft-spoken. That is the differentiation that nothing on TikTok can replicate.

04typography

three voices, one system

The contrast between a literary serif, a handwritten line, and a quiet sans is the whole identity. Same three fonts everywhere. That's how it becomes recognizable.

i · the serif
SaiUniversity.
Fraunces. For titles, essay headers, podcast covers. Literary, slightly old-world, beautifully imperfect at large sizes.
ii · the hand
when did you last
feel understood?
Caveat. For question cards, annotations, captions. The "internet friend" voice. Never the whole sentence. Accents only.
iii · the structure EPISODE 04 · NOTES ON LONELINESS · SAI RUI GE
Inter. For UI, captions, metadata. Quiet. Almost invisible. Lets the serif and the hand do the talking.
05identity marks

two logo routes

Route A is the "main face": profile picture, podcast cover, business card. Route B is the secondary stamp, for headers, watermarks, and future merch. Both live together in the system.

route a · the portrait

A soft pencil-sketch portrait. Same quiet recognition as the Anything Goes mark, but it's you. Works as a profile picture, podcast cover, business card. The main face of the brand.

07content system

Every post slots into one of these. The grid stops being random and starts feeling like a magazine.

Advice Sessions

Soft conversational podcast clips. Mic close-up, warm lighting, vulnerable. The format you already use, just tightened visually.

Your signature format. Identity-driven prompts on textured backgrounds. Engineered for reshares and story reposts.

Substack → TikTok pipeline. Camus, internet criticism, modern loneliness. Serif type, voiceover, slow.

iv

Lifestyle b-roll: matcha at sunrise, palm trees, notebook margins. Builds the world. Earns trust without trying.

09guardrails

bright academic, not dark academic

You said it yourself: "soft, bright, approachable." Everything below either honors that, or kills it.

bright academic
  • warm daylight, ivory paper, blush
  • imperfect, slightly handmade
  • one serif, one hand, one sans
  • quiet, slow, intentional pacing
  • your actual face, your actual voice
  • literary, but never dusty
dark academic (avoid)
  • gothic libraries, moody candles
  • brown / burgundy / forest overload
  • heavy serif spam, blackletter
  • productivity / self-help energy
  • loud podcaster cover art
  • luxury-agency over-polish
